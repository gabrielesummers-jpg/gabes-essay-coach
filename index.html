About Gabe

Warm, practical writing coaching that keeps the work yours.

Gabe is an English writing tutor who helps students revise ethically through thoughtful feedback, coaching, citation guidance, and clear next steps. His goal is to help you understand what is working, what needs attention, and how to move into the next draft with less stress.

Reviews are supportive and direct: you will get guidance on big-picture writing decisions, sentence clarity, source use, and citation details without anyone taking over your paper.

FAQ

Common questions before you submit.

How quickly will I receive feedback?

Basic and Full Essay Reviews are returned within 24-48 hours. Priority Review offers a faster turnaround when Gabe has availability.

What kind of feedback will I receive?

You can expect margin-style comments, summary feedback, a revision checklist, and notes on thesis, organization, source use, citation formatting, grammar patterns, and clarity.

Do you edit or rewrite papers?

No. Gabe identifies patterns, asks guiding questions, explains revision options, and points out areas to strengthen. He does not rewrite sentences or produce text for submission.

Can you help with MLA or APA citations?

Yes. Citation support can include formatting checks, source integration feedback, in-text citation guidance, and notes on Works Cited or References pages.

How does this protect academic integrity?

The feedback is instructional. You remain the author, make the final decisions, and use the review to improve your own draft and writing process.
