FAQ

Common questions before you submit.

How quickly will I receive feedback?

Basic and Full Essay Reviews are returned within 24-48 hours. Priority Review offers a faster written turnaround when I have availability.

What kind of feedback will I receive?

You can expect margin-style comments, summary feedback, a revision checklist, and notes on thesis, organization, paragraph development, source use, citation formatting, grammar patterns, and clarity.

Do you edit or rewrite papers?

No. I identify patterns, ask guiding questions, explain revision options, and point out areas to strengthen. I do not rewrite sentences or produce text for submission.

Can you help with MLA or APA citations?

Yes. I can provide formatting checks, source integration feedback, in-text citation guidance, and notes on Works Cited or References pages.

How does this protect academic integrity?

My feedback is instructional. You remain the author, make the final decisions, and use the review to improve your own draft and writing process.

How do I submit a draft?

Choose Basic, Full, or Priority Review in the pricing section. The matching Tally form will open in a new tab so you can upload your draft, share assignment instructions, and complete payment.

What happens after payment and submission?

After you submit the form, I review the draft and return written feedback through the contact information you provide. If I need clarification about the assignment instructions, I will reach out by email.
